Business & Entrepreneurship: TAR0

TARO is a food delivery service created to increase sources of income for informal workers through a digital marketplace in Phnom Penh, the capital city of Cambodia. Informal workers' businesses have suffered great losses due to the pandemic and the move to a more digital economy. TARO connects customers directly to street vendors in the city, harnessing the exciting culture of street food to open up online business to informal workers. We aim to be the number one platform helping informal workers around the world continue to make a living, one plate at a time!
